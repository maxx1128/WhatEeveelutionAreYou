Which Eeveelution Are You?

In the Pokemon franchise, Eevee is well-known for having more potential evolutions than any other. As of now, it can evolve into one of eight different Eeveelutions, as they're called. That's a lot of potential pokemon to chose from.

Thankfully, this isn't about which one you should evolve your own Eevee into. It's about which one matches your personality the best in this 24-question quiz. While no one's personality is entirely one eeveelution of the other, this will at least show you which one is closest to who you are.

So what're you waiting for? Click the button, answer the questions, and (hopefully) enjoy the quiz!
